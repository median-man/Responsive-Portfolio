About Me

John Desrosiers is an enthusiastic and passionate web developer keen to create useful and interesting appications and websites on a team of like-minded developers.


Currently, John is a full-time student in the Coding Bootcamp at UC San Diego Extension. Before joining the bootcamp, John spent the past ten years working in retail at REI as a member of a diverse team of individuals who together were able to achieve record setting performances for the business unit in between trips together to ski, hike, camp, climb, and otherwise explore the outdoors. His rolls during this time included management, office administration, sales, and customer service. He attended Chapman University where he earned a B.S. in Business Administration prior to his career in retail.
